  • Ways to recognize good sand

    Ways to recognize good sand

    Sand is one of the building materials. Sand is the filler material i.e. filling the gap between the concrete is the work of sand . Masonry and plaster are also made with sand . Its quality, price, and type of work depend on the fineness of the sand . For example, Sylhet sand is the sand that is a little coarse grained and it is used in casting. So before buying sand , you must see if it is of good quality.

    Sand field test:
    • 1. Take a lump of sand in your hand and throw it away, if you see that your hand is not dirty, just a little bit of sand, then you will understand that these are good sand .
    • 2. Take half the amount of sand in a glass bottle and fill it with water leaving one third of the bottle empty and shaking it with the mouth closed. If the sand is good, it will settle as sediment very quickly and if dirt is added, it will look cloudy.
    • 3. If the sand looks angular and sharp to the naked eye, then these are good sand .
    • 4. The sand will remain salt free.
